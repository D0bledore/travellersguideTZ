Tanzania 101 Quiz

1. What is the capital city of Tanzania?

Correct answer: Dodoma

2. Who was the first President of Tanzania?

Correct answer: Julius Nyerere

3. What is the highest peak in Tanzania?

Correct answer: Mount Kilimanjaro

4. Which of the following is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Tanzania?

Correct answer: Both

5. What year did Tanzania gain independence from Britain?

Correct answer: 1961
